Education

We educate our members on the latest advancements in cryptocurrencies and blockchains. We invite blockchain leaders to share their experience with the club.

Conference

We host an annual blockchain conference in New York City, gathering top industry and academic leaders.

Technical Projects

We work on technical projects throughout the semester. In the past, we have worked on Zero-Knowledge Proofs on Ethereum, Programming Language for Ethereum and Bridge between Bitcoin and Ethereum.

Advisory

We work with companies on business problems such as marketing strategy, go-to-market, and community management. We write thought leadership pieces for the blockchain community. Previous projects include consulting for a IoT Rewards Blockchain in India, Presentations on Blockchain Usage in Supply Chains, Cross Border Payments and a project on University Blockchain Talent.
